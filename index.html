My Services

Tile Installation

Tile installation involves a thorough preparation process, where the installer assesses the installation area, measures dimensions, and ensures the substrate is suitable and level. They assist clients in selecting the appropriate type of tile based on factors like durability and aesthetics. During the installation, the installer carefully lays out the tiles in the desired pattern, cutting them to fit edges and corners, applying adhesive, and maintaining precise spacing with grout lines, using specialized tools to achieve a professional finish.

Learn more

Grouting and Sealing

After the tiles are securely set, the installer fills the spaces between them with grout, which not only enhances the visual appeal but also prevents moisture intrusion. The choice of grout—sanded or unsanded—depends on the tile type and joint width. Following grouting, the installer applies a sealant to both the grout and, in some cases, the tiles themselves, protecting the surface from stains and moisture, which is crucial for ensuring the longevity and ease of maintenance of the tiled area.

Learn more

Tile repair and mainatance

Tile repair and maintenance services address common issues such as cracked, chipped, or loose tiles, allowing for the replacement of individual tiles or re-grouting as necessary. Additionally, installers provide routine maintenance services, including deep cleaning, re-sealing, and polishing, to keep the tiles looking their best. They also offer valuable advice on ongoing care, ensuring that the tiled surfaces remain durable and aesthetically pleasing over time.

Granite

Granite tiles are natural stone tiles made from granite, known for their durability, unique patterns, and resistance to heat and scratches, making them a popular choice for flooring and countertops.

floor boards

Floorboards are long, narrow planks made from wood or engineered materials used to create a sturdy and aesthetically pleasing surface for flooring in homes and buildings.

ceramic tiles

Ceramic tiles are durable, water-resistant flooring and wall coverings made from clay that is fired at high temperatures, often available in various colors, patterns, and finishes.
